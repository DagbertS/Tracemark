How This Works

Every AI decision intercepted by Tracemark is recorded as a provenance entry in an append-only store.

Each entry contains an HMAC-SHA256 hash computed over all its fields, plus the hash of the previous entry. This creates an unbreakable chain.

If any historical entry is modified — even a single character — the hash chain breaks and tampering is immediately detectable.

This gives regulators and auditors a cryptographic guarantee that the AI activity log has not been altered, meeting EU AI Act traceability requirements.
